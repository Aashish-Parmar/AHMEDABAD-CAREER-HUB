# üéì Student Role - User Guide & Explanation

## üìã Overview

The **Student Role** is designed for job seekers who are looking for internships and full-time opportunities. As a student on the Ahmedabad Career Hub platform, you can browse job listings, apply to positions that match your interests, track your application status, share interview experiences with the community, and manage your profile.

---

## üöÄ Getting Started

### **Registration**

To get started, you need to create a student account:

1. **Navigate to Registration Page** - Click on "Register" from the home page or login page
2. **Select Student Role** - Choose "Student" when prompted to select your role
3. **Fill Required Information:**
   - **Name**: Your full name (minimum 2 characters)
   - **Email**: A valid email address (must be unique)
   - **Password**: Must be at least 8 characters with:
     - At least one uppercase letter
     - At least one lowercase letter
     - At least one number
   - **College**: Your college/university name (required for students)
4. **Submit** - Your account will be created and you'll be automatically logged in

### **Login**

After registration, you can login anytime using:
- Your registered email address
- Your password

You'll stay logged in across sessions until you manually logout.

### **2. Browse Jobs** (`/jobs`)

Explore available job and internship opportunities:

**Features:**
- **View All Jobs** - See all available positions posted by companies
- **Search Jobs** - Search by job title or keywords
- **Filter Options:**
  - **Job Type**: Filter by "Internship" or "Full-time"
  - **Tech Stack**: Filter by required skills/technologies (e.g., React, Node.js, Python)
- **Job Cards Display:**
  - Job title
  - Company name
  - Job type (Internship/Full-time)
  - Location
  - Required skills
  - Salary/Stipend range
  - Posted date

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to the Jobs page from the navigation menu or dashboard
2. Use the search bar to find specific jobs by title
3. Apply filters to narrow down results based on your preferences
4. Click on any job card to view full details
5. Scroll through pages to see more opportunities

**Tips:**
- Use multiple filters together for better results
- Check back regularly as new jobs are posted frequently
- Save job links for positions you're interested in

---

### **3. Job Details** (`/jobs/:id`)

Get comprehensive information about a specific job:

**What You'll See:**
- **Full Job Description** - Complete details about the role, responsibilities, and requirements
- **Company Information** - Company name, description, and tech stack
- **Job Specifications:**
  - Job type (Internship/Full-time)
  - Location
  - Salary/Stipend
  - Required skills
- **Apply Button** - If you haven't applied yet, you'll see an "Apply" button
- **Application Status** - If you've already applied, you'll see your current status

**How to Use:**
1. Click on any job from the browse jobs page
2. Read through the complete job description carefully
3. Review the required skills and see if you match
4. Check company information to learn more about the organization
5. Click "Apply" if you're interested (make sure you're logged in)
6. You'll receive a confirmation when your application is submitted

**Important Notes:**
- You can only apply once to each job
- Make sure you meet the basic requirements before applying
- You can view the job details multiple times even after applying

### **7. Submit Interview Experience** (`/submit-interview`)

Share your interview experience to help fellow students:

**Why Share:**
- Help other students prepare better
- Contribute to the community
- Share valuable insights about the interview process
- Build your reputation in the community

**What You Can Share:**
- **Company Selection** - Choose the company you interviewed with
- **Interview Date** - When the interview took place
- **Interview Rounds** - Add multiple rounds (Technical, HR, Coding Test, etc.)
  - Round type (e.g., "Technical Round", "HR Round")
  - Questions asked in each round
  - Difficulty rating (1-5 scale)
- **Overall Rating** - Your overall interview experience rating (1-5)
- **Anonymous Option** - Choose to share anonymously if preferred

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to "Submit Interview Experience" from dashboard or menu
2. Select the company from the dropdown (or it may be pre-selected from company page)
3. Enter the interview date
4. Click "Add Round" to add interview rounds
5. For each round:
   - Enter round type
   - List questions asked (one per line or comma-separated)
   - Rate difficulty (1 = Very Easy, 5 = Very Difficult)
6. Add more rounds if applicable
7. Set overall rating for the entire interview experience
8. Choose whether to submit anonymously
9. Click "Submit Interview Experience"

**Tips for Sharing:**
- Be honest and accurate
- Include specific questions asked
- Mention technical questions if applicable
- Rate difficulty fairly
- Help others by being detailed

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I apply to multiple jobs?**
A: Yes! You can apply to as many jobs as you want.

**Q: Can I apply to the same job twice?**
A: No, you can only apply once to each job posting.

**Q: How do I know if my application was received?**
A: You'll see a confirmation message after applying, and the job will appear in "My Applications" with status "Applied".

**Q: How often are application statuses updated?**
A: Statuses are updated by recruiters when they review your application. Check regularly for updates.

**Q: Can I edit or delete my interview experience after submitting?**
A: Currently, you cannot edit or delete submitted interview experiences. Make sure to review before submitting.

**Q: Is my interview experience visible to companies?**
A: Yes, companies can see interview experiences shared for their organization, which helps them understand candidates' experiences.

**Q: What should I do if I forget my password?**
A: Currently, password reset functionality may need to be set up. Contact support if you need assistance.

**Q: Can I change my college name?**
A: Yes, you can update your college name in your profile settings.
